# FinOps: Cloud Financial Operations

> **Master cloud cost management and financial accountability for DevOps teams**

## 🎯 What is FinOps?

FinOps (Financial Operations) is the practice of bringing financial accountability to cloud spending through collaboration between engineering, finance, and business teams. In 2026, with organizations wasting 30-40% of cloud spend, FinOps has become critical for DevOps managers.

## 📊 Market Reality (2026)

- **59% of organizations** have dedicated FinOps teams
- **30-40% typical cloud waste** ($240B+ wasted annually)
- **GPU/AI workloads** burning $10K+/month unnoticed
- **FinOps Engineers** earning $140K-$200K+ (US market)
- **DevOps Managers** now accountable for budget and unit economics

## 💡 Key Concepts

### The FinOps Principles
1. **Teams need to collaborate** - Engineering, Finance, Business
2. **Everyone takes ownership** - Decentralized cost responsibility
3. **A centralized team drives FinOps** - Center of Excellence
4. **Reports should be accessible and timely** - Real-time visibility
5. **Decisions are driven by business value** - Cost vs innovation
6. **Take advantage of the variable cost model** - Cloud flexibility
